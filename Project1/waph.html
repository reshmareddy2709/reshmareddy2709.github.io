Hackathon-1 Overview

The XSS Hackathon, focused on Cross-site Scripting, serves as an educational endeavor with the goal of exploring and understanding various levels of XSS vulnerabilities in web applications. XSS, a common security loophole, enables attackers to inject malicious scripts into web pages, visible to other users. The hackathon involves two main objectives: Task 1 focuses on identifying and exploiting XSS vulnerabilities in a specified web application, while Task 2 revolves around implementing protective measures against XSS attacks to reduce the risk of exploitation.
